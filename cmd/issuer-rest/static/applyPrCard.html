Apply Digital Permanent Resident Card

Step 1

Before starting the application process, there are three questions you should answer first:

1. Are you eligible to apply?
  * You need a valid paper based PR Card
  * You will need a sign-in account

2. Do you have an invitation?
  * At this time, you need an invitation to apply for a digital pr card
  * Your invitation contains a unique code you will need for the application

3. Do you have a Digital Wallet to Store your Digital PR Card?
  * The Digital PR Card will be stored in your Digital Wallet.

If the answer to all three questions is YES:

Apply for your Digital Permanent Resident Card
